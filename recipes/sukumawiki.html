Sukumawiki Recipe

Description

Kales, mostly referred to as sukumawiki by locals is a cheaply available vegetable that is consumed by most households on a daily basis.

Ingredients

  • Kales
  • Red onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tomato
  • Royco cube
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a sufuria, add oil and heat for a while
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown
  3. Add the chopped tomato and cover to cook till soft
  4. Add salt to taste and the royco cube
  5. Add the chopped kales and cover to cook till desired softness
