Ugali, sukumawiki Recipe

Description

Ugali is the staple food in Kenya and a favorite for many nationals. It is quite easy to prepare and only requires a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. In a sufuria, boil water. The amount depends on the number of people 1 cup per person.
  2. Add flour to the boiling water and knead with a mwiko, adding until its to the preferred stiffness.
  3. Give it time to cook before removing from the stove.
